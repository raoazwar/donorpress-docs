The **Gift Aid** module helps UK-registered charities collect Gift Aid declarations from donors and export the data in a format HMRC accepts.

<Info>
	Gift Aid is a **Premium plan** module, designed specifically for UK charities. [See all modules →](/modules/overview)
</Info>

## What it does

Gift Aid is a UK government scheme that lets charities reclaim 25p from HMRC for every £1 a UK taxpayer donates — but only if the donor opts in with a valid declaration.

This module:

- Adds a Gift Aid opt-in checkbox to your donation forms.
- Captures the donor's full name and HMRC-compliant address.
- Stores a permanent record of each declaration.
- Exports declarations in HMRC's [ChR1 schedule format](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/schedule-spreadsheet-to-claim-back-tax-on-gift-aid-donations) for easy claim filing.
- Provides a Gift Aid summary dashboard.

## Form integration

When the module is active and Gift Aid is enabled on a form, donors see:

1. A checkbox: **"I want to Gift Aid this donation and any donations I make in the future or have made in the past 4 years."**
2. The standard HMRC declaration text below the checkbox.
3. Address fields (required if the donor opts in): house number/name, street, city, postcode.

Donors who don't tick the box give as normal — no Gift Aid is recorded.

## Settings

**DonorPress → Settings → Gift Aid**:

| Setting | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Enable Gift Aid | toggle | Master switch. |
| Charity name | text | Used in the declaration text. Must match your HMRC registration. |
| HMRC reference | text | Your charity's HMRC charity reference number. Used in CSV exports. |
| Declaration text | textarea | The legal declaration text shown above the checkbox. Pre-filled with HMRC's wording — only edit if you have legal advice. |
| Auto-enable on new forms | toggle | When you create a new form, default the Gift Aid setting to ON. |
| Address required | toggle | Make full address mandatory when Gift Aid is opted in. (HMRC requires it.) |

## Data captured

Per Gift Aid declaration:

- Donor full name
- House number/name
- Street
- City / Town
- Postcode (validated against UK postcode format)
- Date of declaration
- Donation ID
- Amount

## Reclaim summary

**Reports → Gift Aid** shows:

- Total reclaimable amount in the selected period (donations × 25%).
- Number of donations eligible.
- Number of unique donors.
- Per-donor breakdown.

## CSV export for HMRC

The **Export ChR1 CSV** button in Reports → Gift Aid generates a CSV in the exact format HMRC requires for online schedules:

```csv
Title,First name,Last name,House name or number,Postcode,Aggregated donations,Sponsored event,Donation date,Amount
Mr,Sam,Smith,42,SW1A 1AA,No,No,15/03/2024,100.00
Mrs,Ali,Patel,The Old Vicarage,W1H 5AB,No,No,22/04/2024,50.00
```

Upload this directly to HMRC's online portal as part of your Gift Aid claim.

## Eligibility rules

HMRC has specific eligibility rules. A donation is Gift Aid eligible only if:

- The donor has **paid (or will pay)** at least as much UK income/capital gains tax in the year as the charity will reclaim.
- The donor is a **UK resident** for tax purposes.
- The donation is a **gift** (not a payment for goods or services).
- The donor has provided a **valid declaration**.

DonorPress doesn't enforce eligibility (it can't know the donor's tax situation) — the donor is responsible for opting in only if they're eligible. The declaration text makes this clear.

## Multi-year declarations

A donor's declaration covers the current donation, all future donations, and donations made in the past 4 years (per HMRC's standard wording). DonorPress retroactively flags eligible past donations from the same donor when they declare.

## Cancelling a declaration

If a donor wants to cancel their Gift Aid declaration:

1. Find the donor in the donor admin.
2. Open the **Gift Aid** tab on their detail screen.
3. Click **Cancel declaration**.

The cancellation is timestamped. Future donations from this donor won't be marked Gift Aid eligible until they make a fresh declaration.

## What this module doesn't do

- **Doesn't file the claim for you.** You still upload the CSV to HMRC manually.
- **Doesn't handle Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS)** — that's filed separately and doesn't require donor declarations.
- **Doesn't track tax thresholds** — donors are trusted to opt in only when eligible.
