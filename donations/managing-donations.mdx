The Donations admin screen is where you'll spend most of your day-to-day time. Every donation — one-time or recurring renewal — lands here.

## Anatomy of the list

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **ID** | Internal donation ID — useful for support tickets and webhook investigation. |
| **Donor** | Donor name with a link to the donor record. |
| **Amount** | Donation amount in your currency. Refunded amounts show as a strikethrough. |
| **Status** | Pending, Complete, Failed, Refunded, Abandoned, or Cancelled. |
| **Form** | Which donation form the donor used. |
| **Gateway** | Stripe, PayPal, or another configured gateway. |
| **Date** | When the donation was created (in your site's timezone). |

## Filters

Above the table you'll find filters for:

- **Status** — show only pending, complete, refunded, etc.
- **Form** — show donations from a specific form.
- **Gateway** — show donations from a specific gateway.
- **Date range** — last 7 days, last 30 days, this month, custom range.
- **Search** — searches donor name, email, and donation ID.

Filters combine, so you can quickly find e.g. "all refunded Stripe donations from May".

## Donation statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Pending** | The donor started the form but hasn't completed payment yet. Often abandoned. |
| **Complete** | Payment confirmed by the gateway. Counted toward all reports and totals. |
| **Failed** | Payment was attempted but declined / errored. |
| **Refunded** | Originally complete, then refunded (in full or in part). |
| **Cancelled** | Manually voided from the admin before payment cleared. |
| **Abandoned** | Pending for more than 24 hours with no payment attempt. |

Only **Complete** donations count toward donor totals and revenue reports by default.

## Inspecting a donation

Click any row to open the donation detail screen. You'll see:

- **Summary** — amount, status, gateway, form, donor link.
- **Donor info** — name, email, address (if collected), donor ID.
- **Gateway data** — gateway transaction ID, payment method (last 4 of card, PayPal email, etc.), and a link to the gateway's own dashboard for that transaction.
- **Notes** — internal notes you can leave for your team.
- **Activity log** — full event history: created, gateway response, webhook events, refunds, status changes.

## Refunding a donation

From a donation's detail screen:

<Steps>
	<Step title='Click "Refund"'>
		The refund button only appears for donations that are eligible (status = Complete and the gateway supports refunds).
	</Step>
	<Step title="Choose full or partial">
		For partial refunds, enter the amount.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Add an optional reason">
		Stored in the activity log and (for Stripe) sent to the gateway's refund metadata.
	</Step>
	<Step title="Confirm">
		The refund is sent to the gateway. On success, the donation status updates to **Refunded** and the donor's lifetime total is recalculated.
	</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
	Refunds are not reversible. Once issued through the gateway, the only way to "undo" is to ask the donor to make a fresh donation.
</Warning>

## Exporting

From the donations list, click **Export** to download a CSV of the currently filtered set. The CSV includes every field you see in the list plus donor email, gateway transaction ID, and notes.

Useful for:

- Annual accounting handoffs
- Importing into a CRM (Salesforce NPSP, HubSpot, etc.)
- Reconciliation with your gateway's own export

## Bulk actions

Select rows with the checkbox column for bulk actions:

- **Resend receipt** — re-emails the donation receipt.
- **Mark as cancelled** — for stuck pending donations.
- **Delete** — permanently removes the donation. Use with care — this is irreversible.

## What about offline donations?

Manually recording offline donations (cash, check, bank transfer) requires the [Manual Donations module](/modules/manual-donations).
