DonorPress supports **recurring donations** — donors who give monthly, quarterly, annually, or on any frequency you enable.

## How recurring works

When a donor opts into recurring on a form:

1. The first charge is processed immediately, just like a one-time donation.
2. A **subscription record** is created in DonorPress, linked to a subscription on the gateway side (Stripe Subscription or PayPal Billing Agreement).
3. The gateway processes future charges automatically on the schedule you set.
4. Each successful renewal creates a new **donation record** linked to the subscription.
5. Donors get a renewal receipt by email each time.

## Supported frequencies

Out of the box, you can offer:

- Daily
- Weekly
- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Yearly

You enable per-form which frequencies the donor sees. We recommend starting with **monthly** and **yearly** only — too many options reduces conversion.

## Subscription statuses

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Active** | Renewing on schedule. |
| **Paused** | Temporarily halted. Can be resumed by the donor or admin. |
| **Cancelled** | Permanently stopped. Donor can start a new subscription, but this one won't resume. |
| **Failing** | A renewal payment failed. The gateway will retry per its own dunning rules. |
| **Expired** | Reached the configured bill count (e.g. "12 monthly payments") and finished. |
| **Completed** | Successfully completed all configured payments. |

## Optional bill count

By default, recurring donations continue until cancelled. You can also set a **bill count** per form (e.g. "12 monthly payments and stop") for fixed-term campaigns.

## Failed renewals

When a renewal fails:

1. DonorPress marks the subscription as **Failing**.
2. The gateway retries on its own dunning schedule (Stripe: 3 attempts over a week by default; PayPal: similar).
3. Donor receives an email asking them to update payment details.
4. After the gateway gives up, the subscription moves to **Cancelled**.

You can fully customize the failed-renewal email from **Settings → Email Notifications**.

## Managing subscriptions

From **Donations → Subscriptions** you can:

- Filter by status, gateway, donor, or date.
- Pause a subscription (skips the next renewal).
- Cancel a subscription (stops all future renewals).
- View the full renewal history (every donation tied to the subscription).

## What donors can do

If you've published the [donor dashboard](/donors/donor-dashboard), donors see their active subscriptions and (in current version) view their next renewal date. Cancellation from the dashboard is on the roadmap.

## Webhooks

Subscriptions rely heavily on webhooks for renewal notifications. **Always configure webhooks** for any gateway you use in production:

<Card title="Configure webhooks" icon="bolt" href="/gateways/webhooks">

Without webhooks, renewals will still process, but DonorPress won't know about them until the next page load triggers a manual sync (and that's not reliable).

## Recurring + tax receipts

Each recurring renewal counts as a separate donation for [annual receipt](/modules/annual-receipts) purposes. So a donor giving $25/month for the calendar year ends up with one annual receipt listing 12 individual donations totalling $300.
