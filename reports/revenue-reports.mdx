The Revenue report is the most-used DonorPress report. It's where you answer "how much did we raise?"

## Headline numbers

At the top of the report:

| Metric | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Total revenue | Sum of completed donations in the period. |
| Donations | Count of completed donations. |
| Average donation | Total / count. |
| New donors | First-time donors in the period. |
| % vs. previous period | Comparison vs. equivalent prior window. |

## Time-series chart

A line chart showing revenue per day (or per week / month, depending on the period length). Hover any point to see exact totals.

You can switch the chart between:

- **Total revenue** — all donations.
- **One-time** — non-recurring donations only.
- **Recurring** — subscription renewals only.
- **By gateway** — stacked by Stripe / PayPal / etc.

## Breakdowns

Below the chart, four breakdown tables:

### By gateway

| Gateway | Donations | Revenue | % of total |

Useful for verifying gateway diversification and spotting if one gateway is underperforming.

### By form

| Form | Submissions | Revenue | Avg donation |

Helps identify which forms convert best. Combine with the form performance metrics from [forms reports](/reports/overview) for deeper analysis.

### By campaign

| Campaign | Donations | Revenue | % of goal | Days remaining |

Tracks campaign progress at a glance. Filter further by category or tag if you have many campaigns.

### Top donations

| Donor | Amount | Date | Form | Campaign |

The largest donations in the period. Useful for major-gift outreach (calling top donors to thank them personally).

## Filters

- **Date range** — from / to.
- **Gateway** — limit to a single gateway.
- **Currency** — for multi-currency sites, defaults to your base currency.
- **Form** — limit to a single form.
- **Campaign** — limit to a single campaign.
- **Status** — by default Complete only; can include others.

## Exporting

The **Download CSV** button exports the time-series data plus the per-gateway and per-form breakdowns.

## Use cases

	<Accordion title="End-of-year board report">

## Reconciling with your bank

Revenue in this report is **gross** — before gateway fees. Your actual bank deposits will be lower by Stripe / PayPal fees (~2.9% + $0.30 per transaction for cards).

For net-of-fees reporting, export both the DonorPress CSV and the gateway's payout report and reconcile. A future release will surface fee data directly in DonorPress.
