A **form** in DonorPress is a reusable donation experience: amount selector, donor fields, payment methods, and (optionally) recurring options. You build one in the admin and embed it anywhere using a shortcode.

	<img src="/images/admin/forms-list.png" alt="DonorPress forms list" />
</Frame>

## What lives on a form

| Section | What it controls |
| --- | --- |
| **Amounts** | Preset donation amounts, plus an optional "custom amount" field. |
| **Donor fields** | First name, last name, email, plus optional fields like phone, company, address. |
| **Payment methods** | Which gateways the form offers (Stripe, PayPal, etc.). |
| **Recurring options** | Whether donors can opt into a recurring donation, and at what frequency. |
| **Receipt** | Where the donor goes after a successful payment. |
| **Styling** | Colors, layout, and copy — including a "Donate" button label. |

## Single-step vs. multi-step

DonorPress supports two layouts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
	<Card title="Single-step" icon="rectangle">
		Everything on one screen. Best for low-friction asks where you want donors to see all fields at once.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Multi-step" icon="rectangle-vertical-history">
		Amount → details → payment, split across screens. Higher conversion for longer forms.
	</Card>
</CardGroup>

You can switch between the two at any time without losing your form configuration.

## How a form gets onto your site

Three options:

1. **Shortcode** — `[donorpress_form id="123"]` on any page or post.
2. **Block** — drop the "Donation Form" block in the block editor and pick the form.
3. **Elementor widget** — for sites built with Elementor, drag the "DonorPress Form" widget anywhere.

[Embedding details →](/forms/embedding-forms)

## Display modes

Each embed has two display modes:

- **Inline** — the form renders right where the shortcode/block is.
- **Modal** — a "Donate" button appears; clicking it opens the form in a centered modal.

The modal mode is great for sticky donation buttons in your header, or for landing pages that want one prominent CTA.

## Conversion tips

A few things we've learned from running thousands of donation pages:

<AccordionGroup>
	<Accordion title="Default to a non-trivial amount">
		Setting the default amount to your second-cheapest preset (instead of "custom") raises average donation by 15-25% on most sites.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion title="Offer recurring up front">
		Showing "Make this monthly" as a checkbox above the payment button (rather than after) doubles recurring opt-in on most forms.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion title="Keep donor fields short">
		Every additional field cuts conversion. Phone and company are best left optional or off entirely unless you need them.
	</Accordion>
	<Accordion title="Use a campaign">
		Forms attached to a [campaign](/campaigns/creating-campaigns) with a visible goal bar consistently outperform standalone forms.
	</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
